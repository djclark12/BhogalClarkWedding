  • Indian Celebration

    Friday, March 3rd 2017

    Celebration 6pm
    Bexleyheath Marriott Hotel

    The Celebration will be held at 6pm to 11pm at The Bexleyheath Marriott Hotel located at 1 Broadway, Bexleyheath DA67JZ. We will be having an Indian style Wedding Reception beginning with drinks, followed by dinner and dancing!

    Getting There

    Guests traveling to the venue should arrive at the Bexleyheath Rail Station. The Venue is a 5 minute taxi ride from the station, we advise guests to pre-book their taxi prior to arriving at the station. If taking the bus from the station, take the 422 or B12 from stop BH (Located on Picford Lane).
